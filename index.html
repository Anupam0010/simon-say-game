How to play

  • Click on the start button to begin the game
  • A white box will flash on the screen--click on the box & remember its position.
  • Shortly after, another box will flash. It could be the same box or a different one.
  • Your task is to click the boxes in the correct order:
  • First, click the box that flashed first.
  • Then, click the box that flashed second.

    • Example: Box 1 flashes first, so you click on Box 1.
    When you click it, a green color appears on Box 1.
    Then Box 3 flashes. Before clicking on Box 3, you must first click Box 1 again,
    and then click Box 3.
    Keep following the sequence in the correct order until the game is over.
